Getting Started with Snowl

You've just installed Snowl, an experiment in messaging in the browser.

Access Snowl from the Tools > Snowl menu or the Snowl button in the bottom left corner of your browser window.

Add buttons to your toolbars for quicker access to Snowl features via the View > Toolbars > Customize... menu option.

Subscribe to feeds by clicking the feed icon that appears in the Firefox location bar when you visit a web site with a feed and selecting Snowl from the list of feed readers on the feed preview page.

Subscribe to Twitter or import an OPML file from the Tools > Snowl menu or the Snowl button in the bottom left corner of your browser window.

Read messages in the river, stream, and list views.

Write a message by pressing the write button in any view.

Get involved! Learn more about Snowl, tell us what you think, browse bug reports, report a bug, and check out the source.
